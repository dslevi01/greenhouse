Greenhouse of Remembrance is dedicated to nurturing plants of departed loved ones, helping families to honor memories while promoting sustainable plant care and community connection.

Keep Your Plants Sustainable

Choosing the right houseplants for your region not only keeps your home green and healthy, but also helps protect the environment. Some plants can be invasive or unsustainable outside their natural habitat.

Interesting fact: Did you know that some common indoor plants, if not cared for properly, can drain excessive water resources or harm local ecosystems if planted outdoors?
